FACILITIES



    LOBBY LOUNGE


    Our newly renovated lobby lounge is a unique place to pass your idle time. You can watch people’s movement over a cup of tea or enjoy the company of your partner in this sleek and soothing atmosphere of the lounge. Perhaps you may simply like to sit back and savor your refreshing beverage and light snack, and let your daily stress and tension melt away.





    SWIMMING POOL


    Hotel SeaBreeze rooftop swimming is a welcomed opportunity to savor a private moment with the elements of nature. The view, whether you are looking up to the endless sky or down at the winding city or runway, gives you the sense that you are hanging between the heavens and earth. To unwind, to seep away your anxieties while you bask in the sun or invigorate with a swim or even sit back with a book, we makes it all come true.
    Start the day or cool off in the afternoon with an unhindered view of the airport runway, Changing & shower rooms, life Guard, Jacuzzi, add an inspirational twist with freshly squeezed juices.

    Opening Hours: 6:30 am – 11:00 am, 7 days a week





    DINING HALL


    The hotel’s cuisine is our pride and joy. You may appreciate this first thing in the morning with the abundant buffet that awaits you, which offers homemade cakes, yoghurt, fruit juices, cereals, marmalade and jam.

    The menu for lunch and dinner, which is accompanied with a rich vegetable buffet, are prepared with attention and can be personalised without difficulty.





    CONFERENCE HALL


    The small details always make a grand impression during an event. And for any grand event, a befitting venue has to be hired. Here at Amrokanon, we are committed to little details and our dedicated event manager will ensure your event shines with bounty of beautiful moments. Let us transform our banquet halls into a themed wedding night for you. Our attention to minute details are infectious, from decoration, to preparation of cakes and the accompanying food, we will ensure everything is timed and tastes to perfection.
